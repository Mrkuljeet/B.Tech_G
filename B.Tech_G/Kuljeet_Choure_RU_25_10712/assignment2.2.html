Introduction to Cyber Security

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and digital data from attacks, unauthorized access and damage.

Ethical Hacking is the authorized process of identifying vulnerabilities in systems to improve security. It helps organizations fix weaknesses before cyber criminals exploit them.
